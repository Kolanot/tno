A complex activity is an activity that can be decomposed in more atomic activities. A complex activity consists of at least two other activities (atomic or complex). An example of complex activity is LoadTransportDischarge activity type, which we defined as follows: 1) Loading a certain transport means with a container at an origin 2) Transport of the container using the transport means towards a destination 3) Discharge of the transport means at destination Properties: - A ComplexActivity hasConstituent at least 2 activities, which can be atomic or complex activities
